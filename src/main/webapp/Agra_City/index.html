AGRA THINGS TO DO

History, Culture, Best Places to Visit and Travel Guide of Agra, Major Historical Destination of Uttar Pradesh, India

Agra, located in Uttar Pradesh, India, is world-famous for its historical monuments and rich Mughal heritage. Known as the "City of the Taj," Agra attracts millions of tourists each year, eager to explore its architectural marvels and immerse themselves in its cultural history.
Agra's history is deeply intertwined with the Mughal Empire. It served as the capital under the rule of several Mughal emperors, including Akbar, Jahangir, and Shah Jahan. The city witnessed the construction of some of the most iconic structures in Indian history during this period.
The culture of Agra is a blend of Mughal traditions and contemporary Indian lifestyle. The city is known for its Mughlai cuisine, featuring rich and aromatic dishes like biryani, kebabs, and petha, a famous sweet made from ash gourd. Agra is also renowned for its traditional arts and crafts, including marble inlay work and leather goods.
The best time to visit Agra is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.
